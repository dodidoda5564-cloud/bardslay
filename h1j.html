Welcome to Bardsley School

The Bardsley English Medium Senior Secondary School, Katni was started in the year 1964, with classes from K.G. to Class IV. Gradually, under the able and dedicated leadership of Mrs. Rachael Cooke, the founder of this institution, a beautiful school building consisting of a big hall, staff room, eight classrooms and a laboratory came up and the classes were upgraded up to Class VIII.

Our School History

On 1st January 1988, Rev. Solomon Nelson took over charge as Principal from Miss C. P. Salve. In the year 1989, the school was upgraded to a High School. Again, during 1991â€“92, it achieved the status of a Higher Secondary School.

The school is now a full-fledged Senior Secondary School affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi, from 1st April 1998.

School Management

The School is managed by BECNIJD (Board of Education Church of North India Jabalpur Diocese). Previously, BECNIJD was known as NDBE.

At present, the Rt. Rev. Dr. P. C. Singh, Bishop of Jabalpur Diocese, is the Chairman of the School.

The School is a minority institution which takes no grant from the Government and depends entirely on the fees obtained from the pupils.
